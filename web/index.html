Bonne * Nuit.

With only you set the alarm you can automatically take sleep log.

Android App GitHub

Record your sleep.

Bonne*Nuit records your sleep log automatically. This application is very useful for anyone who wants take sleep log but does not continue. Unlike other similar applications, it automatically detects that you fall asleep and starts recording your sleep log. With only you set the alarm, you can record your sleep log!

Bonne*Nuit は、自動で計測開始する睡眠ログアプリです。 これまでのアプリとは違って、あなたが就寝したことを自動で検知し、睡眠状態の記録を開始します。 毎日かかさず記録を取りつづけるのが苦手なひとでも、簡単に睡眠状態の記録が残せます。
